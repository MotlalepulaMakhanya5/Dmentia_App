How to Use This Application

Step 1: Prediction Form

Start predicting dementia risk on the prediction form. Fill in the form and click on the submit button then wait for some seconds to get your prediction results.

Step 2: View Personalized Recommendations

After completing a prediction, check the recommendations section for tailored advice based on your results.

Step 3: Engage in the Community Forum

Join discussions, share experiences, and learn from others in our community forum.

Step 4: Play the Memory Match Game

Enhance your cognitive skills by playing the Memory Match Game available in the dashboard.

Step 5: Use Chatbot

For any inquiries or support, interact with our chatbot for instant assistance.

Step 6: Update Your Settings

Customize your account settings, change your password, and update your profile as needed.
